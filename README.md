**MoVITex**

AI-Powered Crowd Intelligence & Energy Optimization System for Smart Campuses

**Overview**

Large university campuses function similarly to small cities where thousands of students move between lecture halls, libraries, cafeterias, hostels, and administrative buildings throughout the day. However, campuses often lack a system that can understand and manage this movement efficiently.

This results in frequent congestion in certain locations, inefficient use of infrastructure, and increased energy consumption due to overcrowded buildings.

MoVITex is an intelligent platform designed to analyze campus crowd movement in real time and provide insights that help optimize both mobility and energy usage across campus facilities.

The system visualizes crowd density, predicts congestion, suggests better routes for students, and provides administrators with analytics to improve campus operations.

**Problem Statement**

Modern campuses experience several operational challenges related to crowd movement and infrastructure utilization.

Common issues include:

overcrowded libraries and study spaces

congestion in corridors and pathways during peak hours

inefficient use of campus facilities

uneven building occupancy leading to increased energy consumption

lack of real-time visibility into campus crowd distribution

Currently, most universities do not have systems capable of monitoring and analyzing how people move across campus in real time.

Without such insights, administrators cannot make data-driven decisions to improve infrastructure utilization or energy efficiency.

**Proposed Solution**

MoVITex introduces an intelligent system that analyzes and visualizes campus crowd movement to optimize how people use campus spaces.

The system provides:

a live campus heatmap that displays crowd density across buildings

crowd prediction capabilities that forecast congestion before it occurs

smart routing recommendations to guide students through less crowded paths

student-facing interfaces that help students find available spaces and avoid congestion

administrative dashboards that provide insights into campus crowd patterns

energy optimization insights by balancing building occupancy

By combining real-time analytics with predictive intelligence, MoVITex transforms traditional campuses into data-driven, efficient, and sustainable environments.

**Key Features**

➤ Real-Time Campus Crowd Heatmap

Visualizes crowd density across campus buildings and pathways using dynamic heatmap visualization.

➤ Predictive Crowd Intelligence

Analyzes movement patterns to anticipate congestion before it occurs.

➤ Smart Crowd Routing

Suggests optimized routes that avoid crowded pathways and buildings.

➤ Student Portal

Allows students to view live crowd conditions and make smarter navigation decisions across campus.

➤ Admin Analytics Dashboard

Provides administrators with insights into congestion hotspots, peak hours, and infrastructure utilization.

➤ Energy Optimization

Balances crowd distribution across buildings to reduce peak energy consumption.

➤ Campus Quality Score

Tracks key performance indicators such as congestion reduction and infrastructure efficiency.
